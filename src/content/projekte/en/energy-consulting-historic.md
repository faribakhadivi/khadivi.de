An individual renovation roadmap was created for a listed historic building ensemble. The challenge was to significantly improve energy efficiency while meeting heritage protection requirements.

## Services

- Building survey and energy analysis
- Individual renovation roadmap (iSFP)
- Funding consultation (BAFA/KfW)
- Implementation support
- Coordination with heritage protection authorities

## Result

Phased renovation concept with optimized funding utilization. Planned energy savings of over 60% upon full implementation.
