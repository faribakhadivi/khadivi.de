## Project Description

[Project description to be provided by Fariba]

The 1960s apartment building underwent a comprehensive energy-efficient renovation. Measures included insulation of the entire building envelope, replacement of all windows with triple-glazed elements, and installation of a modern air-to-water heat pump combined with a photovoltaic system.

## Measures

- Complete facade insulation (ETICS)
- Roof insulation
- Basement ceiling insulation
- Window replacement (triple-glazed)
- Air-to-water heat pump
- Photovoltaic system (12 kWp)
- Controlled ventilation with heat recovery

## Result

Reduction of primary energy demand by over 75%. Achievement of KfW Efficiency House 70 standard.
