## Project Description

[Project description to be provided by Fariba]

New construction of a single-family home to KfW Efficiency House 40 standard. The design placed special emphasis on a highly efficient building envelope, the use of a ground-source heat pump, and the integration of smart building technology.

## Measures

- Highly insulated building envelope (U-value < 0.15 W/m²K)
- Ground-source heat pump (brine-to-water)
- Photovoltaic system with battery storage
- Controlled ventilation system
- Smart building controls

## Result

Near energy-autonomous operation. KfW Efficiency House 40 standard achieved.
